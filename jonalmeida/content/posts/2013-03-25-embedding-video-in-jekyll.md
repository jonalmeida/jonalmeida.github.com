### The code:
Youtube embedded code works just as fine in Markdown.<br>

```html
<iframe
  width="560"
  height="315"
  src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/prX3--rtqXk"
  frameborder="0"
  allowfullscreen>
</iframe>
```
